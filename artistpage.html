Dance!

Vocals: IMYNOAH x Production: IGNITE

IMYNoah is back with a new loose track called “Dance.” For this one, he called up one of his favorite producers, Ignite. This song starts out with an extended, unique sample that does a great job setting the atmosphere for the rest of the track. Noah and Ignite have huge plans that will be in full affect sooner than you think, but until then, be sure you tune in to their latest release down below.

GO

Vocals: IMYNOAH x Production: IGNITE

imyNoah and Ignite are here with a new single titled “Go.” Noah’s unique sound with a consistent flow fit Ignite’s instrumental beautifully. Just over a minute and a half in duration, this song sets itself apart from the rest with it’s use of unorthodox samples and to present an overall new sound. The two Columbia College students have big plans for the future as this is only the beginning of everything they have in store. Check out their first collaboration down below!
